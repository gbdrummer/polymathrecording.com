Record your music in a creative, flexible environment at the highest quality

Our full service studio offers the best in recording, mixing and mastering services. Our goal is to provide an environment in which artists can be creative and productive, and to bring out the absolute best in their music.

We've been helping our clients get the most out of their recordings since 2005, when producer, engineer and musician Graham Butler began working with artists in the Nashville area and later Chicago. Since 2012 he's made his home in Los Angeles.
